# Unfixedtime - Brand Guide

## Brand Philosophy

**Sustainability & Harmony**

Unfixedtime embodies a philosophy of sustainable living, mindful design, and harmonious balance. The brand reflects a deep appreciation for natural beauty, thoughtful craftsmanship, and the interconnectedness of our world.

**Modern & Vibrant Expression**

While maintaining our core values of sustainability and harmony, the brand now expresses itself through modern, vibrant design that clearly communicates expertise in product leadership and AI innovation. The visual language is bold, dynamic, and forward-thinking while remaining grounded in natural, sustainable principles.

## Core Values

1. **Sustainability** - Mindful choices that respect our planet
2. **Harmony** - Balance between innovation and tradition, digital and natural
3. **Authenticity** - Genuine stories and real experiences
4. **Thoughtfulness** - Intentional design and careful consideration
5. **Innovation** - Leading with AI and cutting-edge technology
6. **Product Excellence** - Building products that matter

## Typography

### Primary Font
- **Headings**: Inter or System Sans-serif (clean, modern, readable)
- **Body**: System Sans-serif or Merriweather (warm, readable, approachable)

### Font Hierarchy
- **H1**: 48px / 3rem - Bold, impactful
- **H2**: 36px / 2.25rem - Strong, clear
- **H3**: 24px / 1.5rem - Section headers
- **Body**: 16px / 1rem - Comfortable reading
- **Small**: 14px / 0.875rem - Supporting text

## Visual Style

### Design Principles

1. **Organic Shapes** - Soft curves, natural flow over rigid geometry
2. **Generous Whitespace** - Breathing room, clarity, focus
3. **Natural Textures** - Subtle paper, fabric, or organic textures
4. **Earthy Imagery** - Real, authentic photos with natural lighting
5. **Minimalist Aesthetics** - Clean, uncluttered, purposeful

### Imagery Style

- **Photography**: Natural lighting, authentic moments, real experiences
- **Color Grading**: Warm, earthy tones with natural saturation
- **Composition**: Balanced, harmonious framing
- **Subjects**: Nature, architecture, people in authentic settings

## Logo & Identity

### Logo Concept
- Simple, clean wordmark
- Organic, flowing typography
- Optional: Subtle leaf or organic shape accent
- Monochrome versions for versatility

## Spacing System

Based on 8px grid:
- **XS**: 4px
- **S**: 8px
- **M**: 16px
- **L**: 24px
- **XL**: 32px
- **XXL**: 48px
- **XXXL**: 64px

## Component Styles

### Buttons
- Rounded corners (8px radius)
- Generous padding (16px vertical, 24px horizontal)
- Subtle shadows for depth
- Hover states with gentle transitions

### Cards
- Soft shadows
- Rounded corners (12px)
- Generous padding
- Subtle borders or no borders

### Navigation
- Clean, minimal
- Clear hierarchy
- Smooth transitions
- Mobile-friendly

## Sustainability Indicators

Visual elements that reinforce sustainability:
- Natural color palette
- Organic shapes and patterns
- References to nature in imagery
- Clean, efficient design (fast loading, minimal waste)

## Tone of Voice

- **Authentic** - Genuine, real, honest
